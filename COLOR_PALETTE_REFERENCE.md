## ğŸ¯ Usage Guidelines

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text**: `#0f172a` (Dark Slate)
- **Secondary Text**: `#475569` (Slate 600)
- **Tertiary Text**: `#64748b` (Slate 500)
- **Placeholder Text**: `#94a3b8` (Slate 400)

### Background Colors
- **Page Background**: `#ffffff` (White) or `#f8fafc` (Slate 50)
- **Card Background**: `#ffffff` (White)
- **Section Background**: `#f8fafc` (Slate 50)
- **Dark Section**: `#0f172a` (Dark Slate)

### Border Colors
- **Primary Border**: `#e2e8f0` (Slate 200)
- **Hover Border**: `#cbd5e1` (Slate 300)
- **Focus Border**: `#4f46e5` (Primary Indigo)

### Button Colors
- **Primary Button**: `#0f172a` (Dark Slate) with white text
- **Primary Button Hover**: White with `#0f172a` text and `#cbd5e1` border
- **Secondary Button**: Transparent with `#cbd5e1` border
- **Success Button**: `#10b981` (Success Green)
- **Danger Button**: `#ef4444` (Error Red)

### Interactive States
- **Hover**: Slight transform (translateY(-2px)) + shadow
- **Active**: Slight scale (0.98)
- **Focus**: Border color change to `#4f46e5`
- **Disabled**: `#94a3b8` color with reduced opacity
